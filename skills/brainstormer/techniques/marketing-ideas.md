## Creative Marketing Techniques

### 1. Story-Based Marketing

Frameworks:
- **Hero**: Customer is the hero
- **Challenge**: Problem they face
- **Guide**: We're the guide
- **Plan**: Here's how we help

### 2. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

- Limited time offers
- Exclusive access
- Scarcity messaging
- Early bird perks
- Countdown timers

### 3. User-Generated Content

- Photo contests
- Review campaigns
- Hashtag challenges
- Testimonial collection
- Community features

### 4. Referral Programs

Structure ideas:
- Give $X, Get $Y
- Tiered rewards
- Exclusive access
- Social recognition
- Product credits

## Campaign Concept Template

```markdown
## Campaign: [Name]

### Hook
[One-line compelling idea]

### Objective
[What we want to achieve]

### Target Audience
[Who we're talking to]

### Message
[What we're saying]

### Channels
[Where we'll say it]

### Creative Elements
- Visuals: [Description]
- Copy: [Tone/key phrases]
- Format: [Video/Post/Ads...]

### Timeline
- [Date]: [Activity]

### Metrics
- [Metric]: [Target]
```
