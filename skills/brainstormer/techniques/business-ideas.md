# Business Ideas Skill

Generate business models, revenue ideas, and startup concepts.

## When to Use

- User wants to start a business
- Need new revenue streams
- Looking for business model innovation
- Exploring startup ideas

---

## Business Idea Categories

### 1. Revenue Models

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Subscription** | Recurring payments | SaaS, content, clubs |
| **One-time Sale** | Single purchase | Products, one-off services |
| **Freemium** | Free + paid tiers | Apps, tools, games |
| **Marketplace** | Transaction fees | Platforms, exchanges |
| **Licensing** | Use rights fees | IP, software, content |
| **Affiliate** | Commission on sales | Content, recommendations |
| **Advertising** | Pay per view/click | Content sites, apps |
| **Subscription Box** | Physical recurring | Niche products |
| **D2C** | Direct to consumer | Brands, crafts |
| **B2B SaaS** | Business software | Enterprise tools |

### 2. Value Capture Methods

- **Direct**: Customers pay
- **Indirect**: Ads, data, referrals
- **Freemium**: Free base + paid upgrades
- **Consumption-based**: Pay for what you use
- **Outcome-based**: Pay for results
- **Hybrid**: Multiple combined

## Idea Generation Prompts

### For Each Category, Ask:

**Who has this problem?**
- What specific group faces this?
- How big is this group?
- Do they have money to solve it?

**What currently exists?**
- Current solutions?
- What's broken about them?
- Price points?

**What's the hook?**
- Why would someone switch?
- What makes this different?
- What's the "wow" factor?

**How do we reach them?**
- Where do they hang out?
- How do they discover solutions?
- What's the sales process?

---

## Business Model Canvas Questions

### For Each Idea, Answer:

**Value Proposition**
- What problem do we solve?
- What need do we meet?
- What bundle of products/services?

**Customer Segments**
- Who are our target customers?
- Which segments matter most?
- Who are our early adopters?

**Channels**
- How do we reach customers?
- How do we deliver value?
- What partnerships help?

**Customer Relationships**
- How do we acquire customers?
- How do we retain them?
- What community forms?

**Revenue Streams**
- How do customers pay?
- What's the pricing model?
- What are the margins?

**Key Resources**
- What assets are essential?
- What IP or expertise needed?
- What partnerships critical?

**Key Activities**
- What must we do well?
- What activities create value?
- What's our competitive advantage?

**Key Partners**
- Who do we need?
- What can others do?
- What risks do partners reduce?

**Cost Structure**
- What are major costs?
- Fixed vs variable?
- Scale economics?

## Startup Idea Generator

### Template Questions

1. **For [specific group] who [need/problem]**
2. **Our [product/service] provides [benefit]**
3. **Unlike [current alternatives]**
4. **We [key differentiator]**

Example:
> For busy professionals who need healthy meals
> Our meal prep service provides weekly delivered plans
> Unlike HelloFresh
> We use AI to personalize based on biometrics

## Validation Checklist

Before pursuing any idea:

- [ ] Real customers exist?
- [ ] They can/will pay?
- [ ] We can reach them?
- [ ] We can deliver?
- [ ] We're the right team?
- [ ] Timing is right?
- [ ] Can we defend it?
